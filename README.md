# HR-Dashboards
1. Description

The HR Analytics Dashboard is an interactive Power BI report designed to analyze employee attrition, workforce demographics, salary distribution, and job role trends within an organization. The dashboard helps HR teams and business stakeholders identify key factors affecting employee retention and make data-driven decisions.

2. The report provides insights into:

. Employee attrition trends
. Attrition by education, age group, salary slab, and job role
. Workforce demographics
. Average employee age, salary, and years at company
. Department and gender-wise filtering for deeper analysis

This dashboard enables organizations to monitor HR metrics efficiently and improve workforce management strategies.

3. Tech Stack

The dashboard was built using the following tools and technologies:

.Power BI Desktop – Main data visualization platform used for report creation and dashboard development.
.Power Query – Used for data cleaning, transformation, and preprocessing.
.DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) – Used for calculated measures, KPIs, and analytical insights.
.Data Modeling – Relationships established between HR datasets to enable efficient filtering and analysis.
.File Format
.pbix for Power BI project development
.png for dashboard preview images

4. Data Source
The dataset used in this project contains HR employee records including:

. Employee demographics
. Department details
. Education background
. Salary information
. Attrition status
. Job roles
. Years at company

The data was imported into Power BI and transformed using Power Query for analysis and visualization.

5. Features / Highlights
. Business Problem
Organizations often face challenges in understanding employee attrition patterns and workforce trends. HR teams require a centralized dashboard to monitor employee statistics and identify factors contributing to attrition.

6. Goal of the Dashboard
To provide HR professionals with an interactive and analytical dashboard that helps:
. Track employee attrition trends
. Analyze workforce demographics
. Identify high attrition job roles and salary groups
. Support data-driven HR decision making
. Dashboard Highlights
. KPI Metrics
